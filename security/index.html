Security division

Visible deterrence. Clear reporting. Reliable cover.

Security services for organisations that need a professional presence, protection of premises and confidence that incidents will be managed properly.

01

Man guarding

Static guarding for offices, industrial sites, warehouses, retail premises and temporary assignments. Officers can support access control, visitor checks, patrols and incident logs.

02

Mobile patrols

Scheduled or random patrol visits to deter crime, check vulnerable points and provide timestamped site observations.

03

Key holding

Secure key holding and call-out response arrangements for alarm activations, welfare checks and emergency access requirements.

04

Door supervisors

SIA-licensed door supervisors for licensed premises, events and customer-facing venues requiring calm, professional front-of-house control.

05

CCTV monitoring

Monitoring support designed to identify suspicious activity, escalate incidents and maintain records for operational follow-up.

06

Emergency cover

Rapid-response cover can be discussed for last-minute cancellations, urgent risk changes or short notice business requirements.
